# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

This project is maintained by a single independent developer. The community
around it - contributors, users, critics - is expected to engage with honesty
and directness. That's the same standard applied to the code itself.

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of experience level, disability, ethnicity,
gender identity, nationality, or background.

## Standards

**Encouraged:**
- Honest technical disagreement - if the code is wrong, say so specifically
- Respectful challenge of design decisions - with reasoning, not dismissal
- Sharing real-world results - good or bad, both are useful
- Asking questions without assuming the answer is obvious

**Not acceptable:**
- Personal attacks or insults directed at contributors or maintainers
- Harassment of any kind, public or private
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Dismissing contributions on the basis of who made them rather than their merit

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to **jeff@quietfire.ai**.
All complaints will be reviewed. The maintainer reserves the right to remove,
edit, or reject any contribution that does not align with this code of conduct,
and to ban any contributor whose behavior is harmful to the community.
